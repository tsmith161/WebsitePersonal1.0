I was born in Nyack, NY along the Hudson.

This is my favorite place! The location is the Mahwah Museum. When I was a little kid, my dad would bring me here if I behaved. My favorite part about the museum was that it had a model train exhibit and the history of infrastructure in that town as well as an old Erie railroad caboose. To this day it is still my favorite due to nostalgia, my continued love of trains, and seeing the history of an area nearby where I grew up!
